Exhibitions

Exhibitions

We have a huge variation of exhibitions available at our museum, varying from Biology, Robotics, Cosmology and so many more exciting categories!

All exhibitions are a part of the museum's free entrance.

We also offer families a variety of challenging and educational activites. With the "Young Stars" club who meet once a week to explore and experiment with a team of experts for the youngest and a guided tour through the learning lab specifically for student groups, plus great video presentations that will inspire and excite the young scientist' mind.

Guided Tours

Guided Tours

Daily organized guided tours are available at our museum for all different exhibitions and displays.

Hourly pre-organized tours tickets are purchased at the ticket office for 70 NOK,- per person at the entrance of the museum.

If you are looking for a customized tour for your group (6+ person) please send an e-mail to the museum to arrange your tour.
