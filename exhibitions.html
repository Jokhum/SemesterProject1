Exhibitions

Exhibitions

We have a huge variation of exhibitions available at our museum, varying from the world of micro organisms and the human body in Biology and Medicine to the fantastic "The Sky Above Us" exhibition of the night sky in the Cosmology exhibitions space and what we can see and know about the universe around us.

Evolution, Robotics & AI, Ecology

Furthermore we have exciting categories such as Ecology, the learning of the humans impact on the planet and our explorations of trying to create a sustainable future for the entire planet.

Robotics and AI, the information revolution is here and robotics and artificial intelligence are the science of the future.

Evolution, explore the fantastic depths of human origins. Where did we come from and how did we get here? Also dont miss out on the recreation models of the fantastic ancient animals which once wandered the earth.

...and even something for the youngest!

We also offer families a variety of challenging and educational activites. With the "Young Stars" club who meet once a week to explore and experiment with a team of experts for the youngest and a guided tour through the learning lab specifically for student groups, plus great video presentations that will inspire and excite the young scientist' mind.

Guided Tours

Guided Tours

Daily organized guided tours are available at our museum for all different exhibitions and displays.

Hourly pre-organized tours tickets are purchased at the ticket office for 70 NOK,- per person at the entrance of the museum. If you are looking for a customized tour for your group (6+ person) please send an e-mail to the museum to arrange your tour.
